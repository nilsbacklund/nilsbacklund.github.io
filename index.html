About Me

I'm an Engineering Physics student at Chalmers University of Technology, currently finishing my master's in Complex Adaptive Systems. I'm drawn to the intersection of AI, machine learning, and real-world applications.

I've had the chance to work on some fun projects along the way—from building an app to compare electric vehicles, to designing PCBs for an electric race car, to creating AI-powered cybersecurity tools. When I'm not coding or studying, you might find me training for my next Ironman, exploring a new city or cooking with some friends.
